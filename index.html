How It Works

From Requirements
to Results.

Traditional sourcing takes weeks. Dealers Choice collapses that to minutes — while giving you richer candidate data than any manual search could surface.

STEP 01
Define Requirements
Enter job title, required skills, experience level, and location. The AI suggests improvements in real time — standardising titles and correcting location formats before your search runs.
STEP 02
AI Searches LinkedIn
Our system queries LinkedIn's network through a multi-layer pipeline, pulling full profiles including experience, education, skills, and open-to-work status. Up to 100 candidates per search.
STEP 03
Private AI Ranks Every Profile
Each candidate is scored 0–100 by a dedicated AI model with a written explanation. Strengths and concerns are flagged. Hire, Maybe, Pass is assigned. All in your private instance.
STEP 04
Act on Results
View ranked candidates with full profiles, bookmark favourites, export to CSV, and revisit past searches anytime. Optionally trigger The Stack to automate outreach to top matches.
